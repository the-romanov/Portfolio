ANIMATION

This page highlights my animation process, showcasing the key steps involved in bringing ideas to life through motion and storytelling.

Introduction

Animation has always been a powerful medium to tell stories, convey emotions, and capture imagination. I specialise in creating 3D animations that bring ideas to life.

With tools like HitFilm Express, Adobe Premiere Pro, and Adobe Photoshop, I create animations that range from simple motion graphics to fully animated scenes.

Animation is about the articulate design of images. It's not just about creating motion; it's about telling a story in a way that engages the viewer visually and emotionally.

Step 1: Storyboarding

Storyboarding is the first and most crucial step in animation. It's where I sketch out the key moments in the animation, visually planning the scenes before actual animation begins.

This stage allows me to map out the timing, angles, and general flow of the story. Storyboards act as the blueprint for the animation process, ensuring that the scenes are structured well before proceeding.

Step 2: Culture & Norms

In the Western World, we have our own norms. In other places around the world, they have their own norms.

For example, red in the Western World is connoted as danger. In China, red is connoted as good luck.

It is not just colour. It is other symbols like food and traditions. We need to identify those.

Step 3: Animation

The animation stage is where the magic happens! Using tools like Adobe Animate or Blender, I create the full animation, adjusting frame by frame to bring the characters and objects to life.

This stage may also involve syncing the animation with dialogue or sound effects, ensuring everything aligns perfectly.

Step 4: Draft

The draft has been created. This is one of the final processes that brings the animation to life in its final form. I export the animated scenes, ensuring they meet the quality requirements for distribution.

Rendering involves final touches like effects or compositing. The goal is to ensure that the animation looks polished and professional before sharing it with the world.

Conclusion

Animation is a dynamic process that blends creativity. Each step from storyboarding to final rendering plays a crucial role in creating animated content.

Through the combination of planning, execution, and technical skill, animation allows me to bring ideas to life.
