3D MODELLING

This page showcases my experience in 3D modeling, detailing the steps I follow to create stunning 3D assets from concept to completion.

Introduction

My journey in 3D modeling began with a fascination for digital art and design. I was captivated by the ability to create my very own models using 3D software.

Over the years, I honed my skills in tools like Autodesk 3DS Max, Sketchfab, and Unreal Engine 5 to create everything from detailed models to fully animated scenes.

3D modeling is both an art and a science. It requires creativity, technical skill, and a deep understanding of geometry and texturing techniques to bring an object to life in the digital world.

Step 1: Conceptualisation

The first step in 3D modeling is conceptualisation. This phase involves gathering reference images, sketches, and inspirations to understand the form and details of the object you want to create.

Before we start making said product, we need to be informed of the client brief which will be our stepping stones.

Step 2: Character Modelling

The modeling phase is where the object takes shape. I begin by creating a base mesh using modelling techniques. The goal here is to define the overall form of the object or character.

Once the base model is established, I refine the shape by adding more detail and subdividing the mesh to make it smoother.

Depending on the complexity, I use certain tools like Extrude, Inset & Chamfer for finer details. This allows me to create highly detailed and organic models with a high level of realism.

Step 3: Scene Modelling

After the model is created, the next step is scene creation. This is where I add a small environment to make the scene a little bit more lively.

I use the same techniques to craft the 3D model. We are taking a remix rather than a spontaneous amalgamation.

Step 4: Map Modelling

Making my scene come to life requires a map.

Conclusion

3D modeling is an intricate process that combines artistic creativity with technical knowledge. It requires patience, attention to detail, and a passion for creating stunning visuals.

From conceptualisation to the final render, I strive to create realistic, detailed, and engaging 3D models that bring ideas to life in the digital world.
